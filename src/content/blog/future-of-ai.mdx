## **What is SkyThought?**

SkyThought is an open-source AI project spearheaded by NovaSky AI, with its flagship model, **Sky-T1-32B-Preview**, positioned as a cutting-edge reasoning model. The project is part of a broader movement to democratize AI by making advanced tools accessible to developers, researchers, and organizations worldwide.

Sky-T1-32B-Preview is particularly notable for its **performance parity with o1-preview**, a leading model in reasoning and coding benchmarks, while maintaining a fully open-source framework. This combination of high performance and accessibility makes SkyThought a compelling choice for those looking to leverage advanced AI capabilities without the constraints of proprietary systems.

---

## **Key Features and Technical Highlights**

SkyThought’s technical foundation is a testament to modern AI engineering. Here’s a closer look at what powers this model:

### **1. Training Process**

- **Dataset**: Sky-T1-32B-Preview was trained on **17,000 data points**, with a focus on science and riddles, incorporating elements from the STILL-2 model.
- **Framework**: The training utilized the **Llama-Factory framework**, running for 3 epochs with a learning rate of 1e-5 and a batch size of 96. The process was completed in just **19 hours** using 8 H100 GPUs, thanks to **DeepSpeed Zero-3 offloading**.
- **Cost Efficiency**: The training cost was approximately **$450**, highlighting the model’s cost-effective development.

### **2. Performance Benchmarks**

Sky-T1-32B-Preview has demonstrated competitive performance across several benchmarks:

- **Math500**: Scored **82.4**, outperforming Qwen-2.5-32B-Instruct (76.2).
- **AIME2024**: Achieved a score of **43.3**, significantly higher than Qwen-2.5-32B-Instruct’s 16.7.
- **LiveCodeBench**: Performance varied by difficulty, with scores of **86.3 (Easy)**, **56.8 (Medium)**, and **17.9 (Hard)**.
- **GPQA-Diamond**: Scored **56.8**, showcasing its reasoning capabilities.

### **3. Open-Source Commitment**

SkyThought’s open-source nature is one of its defining features. The project provides access to:

- **Model weights**
- **Training scripts**
- **Evaluation tools**
  This transparency fosters collaboration and innovation, allowing the community to build upon the model’s foundation.

### **4. Community and Collaboration**

The project is supported by the **Berkeley Sky Computing Lab**, with contributions from **Lambda Labs**, **Anyscale**, and the **Still-2 Team**. This collaborative effort underscores SkyThought’s integration within the broader AI research ecosystem.
